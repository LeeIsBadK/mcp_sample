# Return & Refund Policy

**Return window**

* Most products: **within 14 days** after the parcel is delivered
* **Exceptions:** Electronics, Mobile Phones, Tablets & Gadgets, and Appliances: **within 7 days** after delivery

---

## Return Conditions

To be eligible for return, the item(s) and packaging must follow the guidelines below.

### Matrix of Conditions

| **Return Reasons**                                                                    | **Sealed Condition**<br/>(plastic seal intact; sticker not peeled) | **New Condition**<br/>(never used) | **All Accessories Returned**<br/>(free gift, manual, accessories, original box) | **Tags & Labels Attached** |
| ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | -----------------------------------------------------------------: | ---------------------------------: | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------: | -------------------------: |
| Defective, Damaged                                                                    |                                                      Not necessary |                      Not necessary |                                                                               ✓ |                          ✓ |
| Expired, Missing item, Not as advertised, Incorrect item delivered, Size does not fit |                           Not necessary *(except Health & Beauty)* |                                  ✓ |                                                                               ✓ |                          ✓ |
| Change of mind                                                                        |                                                                  ✓ |                                  ✓ |                                                                               ✓ |                          ✓ |

**“Change of mind”** means the customer decides not to keep the item(s) for reasons other than: Defective, Damaged, Expired, Missing item, Not as advertised, Incorrect item delivered, or Size does not fit.
Items eligible under “Change of mind” must be **unused**, in their **original condition**, and in the **original packaging**.

## Category-Specific Guidelines

### Fashion

* Earrings **cannot be returned**.
* Accessories & Jewelry that are **made to order** **cannot be returned**.
* **Swimwear** must be tried on over underwear **without removing** the hygienic strip. If removed or tampered with, **not eligible** for return. Items must be in original condition, unworn, unaltered, unwashed, with all tags attached.
* **Underwear (bras)** must be in original condition, unworn, unaltered, unwashed, with all tags attached. **Underpants** and **bra sets with underpants** **cannot be returned**.
* **Fashion clothes** may be tried on. Returns must be in original condition, unworn, unaltered, unwashed, with all tags attached.
* **Footwear** must be in original condition; **shoe soles must be clean and unused**.
* **Watches, Eyeglasses, and Accessories** must be returned in original, unaltered condition with all tags and original packaging.

### Health & Beauty

* **Cosmetics, skincare, and personal care** products must be **sealed** in original condition with all tags and original packaging.

### Mom & Kids

* **Breast pump, breast pads, baby bottles, storage bags, rubber teats/nipples, baby wipes, and cleaners** must be **unused** and in **original condition**.

### Pets

* **Pet food, pet toys, pet grooming, fish & aquatic supplies, pet cleaning products** must be **unused** and in **original condition**, with original packaging and all tags attached.

### Sports

* **Training equipment, bicycles, boxing gloves, sandbags** must be **unused** and in **original condition**, with original packaging and tags attached.
* **Food supplements** must be **unused** and in **original condition**, with original packaging and tags attached.
* **Massage chairs** that are **made to order** **cannot be returned**.

### Books, Games & Music

* **Books, movies, DVDs, software** must be **unused** and in **original condition**, with original packaging and tags attached.

### Automotive & Tools

* **Oils & lubricants, car care/cleaning products, air refreshers** must be **unused** and in **original condition**, with original packaging and tags attached.

### Office Equipment

* **Made-to-order and customized products** **cannot be returned**.
* **Tables/wardrobes/oversized furniture** and **furniture assembled at the delivery point** **cannot be returned**.

### Electronics, Mobile Phones, Tablets & Gadgets, Appliances

* **Opened or seal-broken items** **cannot be returned**.
* Items where the **warranty card has been filled in by the customer** **cannot be returned**.

### Others (Non-returnable)

* **Free gifts, gift cards, e-codes, e-coupons, chemical products, food & beverages, clearance items** **cannot be returned**.

**Remark:** If *The 1* points were used as a cash discount together with another payment method, the points will be refunded to your *The 1* account first, and the remaining amount will be refunded via the **same payment method** used for purchase.
